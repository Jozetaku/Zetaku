Our Approach

At Zetaku, we combine traditional Thai massage techniques with specialized wooden therapy tools to help release deep muscular tension and support overall wellbeing.

Our work is designed for professionals who carry tension from long hours at a desk, active travellers recovering from the Alps, as well as those experiencing mental and physical fatigue. We focus on delivering effective pressure while maintaining comfort and care.

Mai Prap Marn

Signature Deep Tissue Release

Specialized Wooden Therapy Tools

The Mai Prap Marn tools allow us to apply deep, precise pressure to areas of chronic tension more effectively than hands alone.

Who is this treatment suitable for?

  • Professionals with desk-related tension
  • Active individuals experiencing deep muscle tightness
  • Those seeking deeper release with attentive care
Note: Due to the depth of pressure applied, some clients may experience temporary marks on the skin. These marks vary between individuals and typically fade naturally over time.
